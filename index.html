Message Container [Video] [Site]


From the beginning of the idea, we thought about the relationship between people and messages; in our opinion, the sound of breathing represents the uniqueness of the message from each person. As a result, we built an interactive sofa that can be a container of this type of message. As the people lie on the sofa, the sound of breathing will play through the speakers. At the same time, the airbag will be inflated by the motor with the same frequency as the breathing. Therefore, people will be able to sense a message by hearing and touching.

Yu-Yu [Video]


The concept of Yu-Yu comes from traditional Chinese pasties, which convey wishes and stories through their beautiful and meaningful patterns. As a popular gift for festival, food is not only for human cosumption but also plays a role of social message carrier.

Yu-Yu service aims to open up the potential of food as messenger by combining customized AR message with patterns on pasties. By means of provding users a better gift experience, Yu-Yu anticipates enriching the gift givers interaction with reciever.

Limbcare [Video]


The concept of Limbcare is based on my personal experience about sports injury. We built up a intelligent packing bag that provide hot and cold therapy to sooth the ankle sprains. We also provide the Ios app that can easily control our packing bag and record the details of the injuries. We plan to cooperate with physical thereapist and doctor to record more data to help the athlete recover.

Oculomotor Drive [Video]


The concept of the 'Oculomotor Drive' is based on my personal experience about eye fatigue. Thus, I am thinking about to gamify the eyes exercise. Say something, please [Video] [Demo]


The concept of the 'Say Something, Please' is that we trying to explore the relationship between message, sound and light, we use the feature of the resonance speaker to produce the sound by vibrate the installation, and because of the vibration, the water inside the installation will produce the ripple effect that can project to the wall by lamp. We also built up a real-time app that provide the user to type the message what they want to say. Therefore, the user can easily interact with the installations by use smart phone.
